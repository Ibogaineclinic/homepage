Ibogaine Detox Your Path to Freedom

Every addiction has two parts. The first is psychological, as traumatic events in your past manifest themselves in the form of destructive behavior patterns. The second is physical, as your addiction alters the dopamine uptake pathways in your brain. Dealing with the emotional issue is what will help in the long run, but first your neurochemistry must be restored so that you can think clearly and feel healthy while completing your healing process. Neurochemistry When Ibogaine enters your bloodstream, it begins to quickly change the dopamine uptake pathways that were damaged by your addiction. This changes the brain processes that motivate your addiction and can change some of the ingrained, compulsive behaviors of addiction. Psychology If the body is detoxified, but the emotional issues are not addressed, patients will soon find themselves once again seeking solace in drugs or alcohol. Our team is here to drive the psychological issues fueling your addiction out of the shadows of your subconscious and into the light of your conscious mind, where you can combat and defeat them.
